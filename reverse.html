YVAN CHIRADE GALLERYYYY ME!?!



REVERSE

Color, stereo sound, 0'27, 2024



Capturing the beauty and diversity of nature through images and videos, highlighting the importance of preserving our natural environment is what Reverse expresses.
The video is made in the context of an exercise on a specific theme : "Exposing nature"

In this video project, the overlays and transparency create depth and a visual complexity.
The whole video is made of different transitions using glitches and cuts.
